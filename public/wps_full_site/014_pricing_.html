      Frequently Asked Questions

      Subscription

      I've never subscribed to WPS Pro before. How can I get a free premium trial, or subscribe the WPS Pro?

      1. Go to WPS official website to choose a WPS Pro plan as required. You can also click the '' button on the WPS Office on various devices or platforms (such as Windows PCs, mobile phones, Macs, and tablets) to choose a plan.

      2. If you've never subscribed to WPS Pro before, you can try premium trial to enjoy all benefits of WPS Pro for free. When the trial ends, you need to pay for the subscription. However, you're free to cancel the subscription at any time during the trial period. The trial duration depends on the service you purchase.

      3. Thank you for your support. If you have any problems when using WPS Office, feel free to contact us at any time by email at officesupport@wps.com .

      I have canceled the auto-renewal service. How can I restart my subscription?

      1. If your subscription is still valid after you cancel the auto-renewal service, go to WPS official website or sign into WPS Office on your devices (such as Windows PCs, mobiles phones, Macs, and tablets). Then you can go to My Subscription page to enable the auto-renewal service.

      2. If the remaining subscription has expired after you cancel the renewal service, please try to resubscribe.

      3. Thank you for your support. If you have any problems when using WPS Office, please feel free to contact us at any time by email at officesupport@wps.com .

      What are the differences between WPS Standard and WPS Pro?

      1. You can go to WPS official website to check the differences between WPS Standard and WPS Pro. You can also click the 'Upgrade Now' button on the WPS Office on various devices or platforms (such as Windows PCs, mobile phones, Macs, and tablets) to check the differences.

      2. Thank you for your support. If you have any problems when using WPS Office, please feel free to contact us at any time by email at officesupport@wps.com .

      Payment

      Why did I fail when paying for the WPS Pro?

      1. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

      2. The first step is to ensure your network works well, and refresh it to pay again.

      3. Check whether your payment method and payment account information are correct. If both are correct, ensure that the balance in your payment account is enough.

      4. If the payment still fails, please send the error messages, screenshots, and user ID (click your profile photo to find the user ID) to us by email at officesupport@wps.com . We will find the solutions for you and help you as soon as possible. Thank you for your support.

      How can I switch the card for payment?

      1. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

      2. At the moment, WPS Office does not support one-click bank card switch. The way to switch cards depends on your payment method. Therefore, check your payment method first, and then go to the payment platform to switch the bound card. If you have any problems, please feel free to contact us by email at officesupport@wps.com . We will solve these problems for you as soon as possible.

      How can I delete an unpaid order?

      1. WPS Office for Android: Go to 'Settings', locate WPS Office in the application list, clear its cache and restart WPS Office. Then, the record of the unpaid order will be deleted.

      2. WPS Office for Windows PC: At the moment, we don't support deleting an unpaid order.

      3. Please be assured that WPS Office will not charge you before you complete the payment.

      Why did I fail when using UPI payment?

      1. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

      2. WPS Office does not support direct payment using UPI. You can add the UPI to your Google Play account and then return to the WPS Pro purchase page. Then you can finish purchase using UPI payment in Google Play.

      3. If you fail to add the UPI to your Google Play account, we recommend that you try other payment methods. WPS Office supports multiple payment methods, including credit or debit card payment, Paypal, etc.

      4. If the problem persists, please send the screenshots or screen recordings to us by email at officesupport@wps.com . We will find the solutions for you as soon as possible. Thank you for your support.

      What payment methods are supported?

      1. Official Website: supports 2checkout, PayPal, etc.

      2. WPS Office for Android: supports Google Play, Paypal, and credit cards.

      3. WPS Office for Windows: supports 2checkout, PayPal, payTM, and credit cards.

      4. WPS Office for Mac: supports Apple Pay, PayPal, and credit cards.

      5. Choose the most suitable payment method as required. More payment methods will be available in your region or country.

      After-sales

      How can I check my account information?

      1. If you want to check your account information after purchase, please click your profile photo or contact the customer service by email at officesupport@wps.com . We will find solutions for you as soon as possible.

      Can I use the subscribed WPS Pro across devices?

      1. You can sign in to the WPS Pro account on multiple devices, including Windows PCs, mobile phones, Macs, and tablets. A maximum of 9 associated devices (3 PCs and 6 mobile devices) are supported.

      2. You can enjoy WPS Pro benefits on different devices with only one WPS Pro account.

      3. Please note that license keys can only be used on Windows PCs.

      Why did I fail to get a WPS Pro free trial?

      1. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

      2. If you have never subscribed to WPS Pro before,please check your network and ensure that the account you use to subscribe is valid and has enough balance. You cannot get a WPS Pro free trial using an invalid account.

      3. If you have subscribed to WPS Pro before, you cannot get a WPS Pro free trial. WPS Pro free trial is only available to new users.The trial duration depends on the service you purchase.Thank you for your support.

      4. After successful subscription, you can get a free trial to enjoy all benefits of WPS Pro. When the trial ends, you need to pay for the subscription. You're free to cancel the subscription at any time during the trial period. The trial duration depends on the service you purchase.

      5. If the problem persists, please send your WPS ID and the screenshots or screen recordings to us by email at officesupport@wps.com officesupport@wps.com . We will analyze it and find the solutions for you as soon as possible. Thank you for your support.

      How can I get an invoice after I subscribed WPS Pro?

      1. If you need an invoice for the service you purchased, please feel free to contact the customer service by email at officesupport@wps.com and provide your order number and WPS ID. We will provide you with the invoice as soon as possible. Thank you for your support.

      Why are there still advertisements after I subscribed the WPS Pro?

      1. We apologize for any inconvenience caused.

      2. Check your subscription status. If it shows non-Premium status, it is likely that you have signed in with a wrong account or the WPS Pro you subscribed has expired. In this case, sign in with the account you have subscribed WPS Pro for, or resubscribe WPS Pro and try again.

      3. If your subscription status shows that you are a WPS Pro user, we suggest that you update WPS Office to the latest version and try again after clearing cache in Settings. Note: Back up all your files before clearing the cache.

      4. If the problem persists, please send your WPS ID and the screenshots or screen recordings to us by email at officesupport@wps.com . We will analyze it and find the solutions for you as soon as possible. Thank you for your support.
